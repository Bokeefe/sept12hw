This is me fixing someone's shower that they mangled. Fecal explosions, getting shocked working on
wall sockets, dizzy spells from paint fumes were common features of my job. I worked as a maintenance
guy for the last five years. I learned a lot and got to be alone most days listening to podcasts but
it was time to try something new.

My girlfriend Gwen and I saw opportunity out west so we bought a wagon.

I filled it with only tunes from its birthyear:1975

We loaded it with everything we own including our guinea pig and took off.

It only made it 44 miles before the radiator blew.

Determined at our new life out west we took the escape pod with our tent.

The second we pulled into Denver my tire blew but we made it.

It was all worth it.

Eventually the mechanics back in Iowa built a new radiator from scratch for the RV. I went back
and got it last week with all the rest of our stuff. At one point some flaming object came in through
the air vent and caught the carpet on fire, which I put out with my feet while driving. Aside from
that, it drove like a dream.
