Dream #1

It was night time and I was running from some Blade Runner-type villans but still found myself
cornered by one. He was summoning some power with his hands to be used on me, I thought to myself:
"You are powerless"...but in my panic I ended up screaming it aloud. As the words came out, my
voice was extremely powerful and demonic, it froze this sci fi soldier in his tracks.

Unfortunately, the dream went on to have me try and use my new superpowers on helpless pedestrians
In some suburban restraunt I tried freezing a waiter with no success. My attempts became more pathetic
and my ego was rightfully deflated.
